Bánh Mì

This delicious bánh mì Vietnamese sandwich is made with chicken and pickled vegtables.

Ingredients

  • French baguette bread
  • Mayonnaise
  • Maggi seasoning sauce
  • Chicken
  • Pickled daikon
  • Pickled carrot
  • Pickled onion
  • Cucumber
  • Cilantro
  • Jalapeno
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Combine rice vinegar, sugar and water in a saucepan. Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring for 1 minute until sugar has dissolved. Let mixture cool.
  2. Place carrot, radish and onion in a bowl. Pour cooled vinegar mixture and let marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. While vegtables marinate, set an oven rack about 6 inches from heat source and preheat the broiler.
  4. Sprinkle chicken breast with garlic salt and pepper.
  5. Broil chicken in pan and turn once until browned and fully cooked. (About 6 minutes per side)
  6. Place chicken on cutting board and cut into bite-sized pieces.
  7. Slice baguette in half lengthwise. Pull out the soft center of the bread.
  8. Lightly toast baguette under the broiler for 2 to 3 minutes.
  9. Drain off excess vinegar mixture after vegtables have marinated.
  10. Assemble the sandwich. Spread each half of the toasted baguette with mayonnaise. Layer chicken on the bottom and top with your pickled vegtables, cliantro and jalapeno. Squeeze lime wedge over fillings.
  11. Cut the sandwich and enjoy!
